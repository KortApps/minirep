Privacy Policy

DRAFT — last edited June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information MiniRep ("we," "us," "the app") collects, how it's used, and what choices you have. It applies to anyone who uses the app or this website.

1. Information we collect

Depending on which features you use, we may collect:

  • Account information: email address, used to create your account and manage your subscription.
  • Usage and activity data: the workouts you complete, your feedback taps (too easy / felt right / too much), streaks, and progress — used to personalize your daily plan.
  • Payment information: handled entirely by Apple/Google's payment systems (or Stripe, if billed via the web) — we do not directly store your card details.
  • Device and technical data: basic information like device type and app version, used for troubleshooting.

2. How we use this information

  • To run the core features of the app — generating your daily workout and adjusting it based on your feedback.
  • To manage your subscription and free trial.
  • To fix bugs and improve the app over time.
  • To contact you about your account or trial status (e.g. a reminder before your trial ends).

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Health-related information

This app may collect information related to your physical activity and self-reported how exercises felt (e.g. pain or discomfort flags). This is considered sensitive information under some privacy laws, including GDPR. We use it only to personalize your plan and will not share it with third parties without your consent. [This section needs specific legal review given the health-adjacent nature of the data — Google Play has separate health app declaration requirements that may apply.]

4. Your rights (especially if you're in the EU/EEA)

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email listed on our Support page.

5. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide the service. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

We may use third-party services for analytics, crash reporting, payment processing, or AI-related features. [List specific services once finalized — e.g. Firebase, RevenueCat, Stripe, any AI model providers — both Apple and Google require these to be explicitly disclosed.]

This app is not directed at children under 13 (or 16, depending on jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via the app or email.

9. Contact us
