Common questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store or Google Play, not directly through the app. You can cancel any time from your phone's subscription settings — your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple or Google directly, according to their own policies, since they process the payment.

Something hurt during a workout — what should I do?

Stop the movement and rest. The app isn't a substitute for medical advice — if pain persists for more than a few days, please check in with a doctor or physiotherapist.
